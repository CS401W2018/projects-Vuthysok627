Why You Should Visit Siem Reab!

Angkor Wat is one of the reasons you should visit Siem Reab! Angkor Wat is well-known for its architecture and history. Angkor Wat became a world-heritage site in 1992. 2.2 million people visit Angkor Wat annually. Besides the Angkor Wat site, Siem Reab has a lot of attractive places, such as Phnom Kulen, a place to see a beautiful sunset, and a night market. You should plan to stay at least one week to see all the places.
