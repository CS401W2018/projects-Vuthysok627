Sihanoukvill City -- The Rising Star

If you want to find a best ocean in Cambodia, Sihanoukvill is the best choice. Sihanoukvill city is well-known for beautiful beaches and see foods. Only 4 hours from Phnom Penh city. Both local and international can experience white sand and clear blue water over there. There many seafood that you can buy to enjoy the view with family or friend. It's great place of weekend or vocation.
