Craving Food?

Visiting Phnom Penh city is incomplete without trying authentic Cambodian dishes. Dishes like amok, made with a mix of meats and rich spices like lemongrass, galangal, and turmeric, are unforgettable. The taste is unmatched and highly recommended for every visitor!
