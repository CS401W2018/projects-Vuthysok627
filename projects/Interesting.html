Watching the stars

There’s something incredibly humbling about looking up at the night sky and seeing distant planets and constellations. Stargazing allows me to slow down and reflect, making me feel connected to something larger than myself. It’s a peaceful activity that contrasts with the fast pace of daily life, and it reminds me of the vastness of the universe.
