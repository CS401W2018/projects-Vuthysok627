Mom is My Hero

Interests Hero

Loving and Caring Mom!

When I was young, I had a high fever that caused my whole body to break out in a rash. Without sleeping the whole night, Mom took care of me until I got better. That memory is still vivid in my mind. Mom is my hero and inspiration. Mom always faces challenges with optimism. She rarely complains about her life. Although she was born and raised in a poor family and never had a chance to go to school, She endeavored to earn money to raise me and my siblings, with a good life. Her love for the family is uncoditional. She's my hero and my motivaiton.

Three Things that Mom is My Hero
Brave
- She always has confident and fearless to overcome all challenges to accomplish her dream.
Optimstic
- She rarely complains or excuse others when she faces challenges in her life.
Charity
.
- She always makes time to serve and reach out to other people in needs.
